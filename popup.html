Amazon Price & Tariff Guesser

This extension helps you compare prices on Amazon.com with their Canadian counterparts. By converting Canadian prices into US dollars using live exchange rates, it gives you an estimate of tariff and other charges that you might pay in the US but not in Canada. In essence, it lets you “guess” the hidden tariff costs and see if cross-border shopping might be more affordable.

How It Works:

1. When you view your cart on Amazon.com, the extension scrapes product details and locates the matching product pages on Amazon.ca.

2. It then extracts the Canadian price from the Amazon.ca page using multiple layout checks and retrieves the latest exchange rate.

3. Using that rate, the extension converts the Canadian price into US dollars. The displayed US value helps you estimate the hidden tariff charges and extra costs.

Use this tool to make smarter decisions on cross-border purchases by understanding and anticipating extra costs.
